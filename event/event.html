ALUMINI AMONG US

We are happy to tell you that E-cell of IIIT Sonepat is organising a webinar for interaction with our alumni, Mr Kunal Sachdeva Sir. He graduated in 2020 and is now placed in Flipkart as Software Developer. He will be sharing various information related to targeting on-campus or off-campus placements, important skills required in the outer world and experiences from his college life.

It will be an open session with him, you can ask him questions(appropriate) and get your queries resolved.

Some of the questions which will be discussed will be:-

1. What to do at current phase.

2. How to target for offcampus or oncampus placements.

3. Skills required today.

4. His batch placements.

5. His experience in college life.

Note:- You are allowed to ask your own queries during the webinar.

Webinar will be on 24th April Saturday at 5 pm.

Webinar will be on Cisco Webex platform.

For further updates please follow E-cell's Instagram and linkedin account
